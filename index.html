About Us

At Nasma, we are revolutionizing healthcare with cutting-edge wearable technology designed to enhance patient monitoring and improve treatment outcomes. Our advanced device continuously collects vital health data, including respiratory rate, sleep quality, heart rate, oxygen saturation, body temperature, and cough index. With real-time alerts, healthcare providers are immediately notified of any abnormal changes in a patient’s condition, enabling timely intervention. Our intuitive dashboard presents structured health insights through detailed reports and visual analytics, making it easier for doctors to analyze patient data. Powered by AI-driven analysis, we provide personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient’s needs. Additionally, our system includes medication reminders to enhance adherence to prescribed treatments. At Nasma, we are committed to transforming healthcare through smart, data-driven solutions that empower both patients and medical professionals

Our Product

Nasma’s Smart Asthmatic Monitoring Chest Patch

Real-time asthma monitoring for better management.

Nasma’s Smart Asthmatic Monitoring Chest Patch tracks key respiratory metrics, providing real-time feedback to help manage asthma effectively. It ensures timely interventions and personalized care for better health outcomes.

Cough Detection

Combines acoustic and kinematic sensing with Edge AI for precise cough detection, providing immediate, accurate results while preserving privacy.

Real-Time Health Monitoring

Tracks heart rate, oxygen saturation, respiratory rate, temperature, and more.

Sleep Monitoring

Monitors sleep quality, detecting disruptions that could indicate asthma flare-ups or other respiratory issues.

Treatment Feedback

Helps adjust tailored treatment plans dynamically, ensuring timely interventions and ongoing management.
